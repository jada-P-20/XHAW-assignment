About Empowering the Nation

Our Mission

Founded by Precious Radebe in 2022, Empowering the Nation provides essential skills training for domestic workers and gardeners who never had the opportunity for formal education or upskilling.

Our programs make participants more marketable, enabling higher wages and entrepreneurial opportunities. We've successfully trained hundreds of individuals through our comprehensive programs.
